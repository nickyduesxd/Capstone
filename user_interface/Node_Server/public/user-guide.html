User Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the User Guide! This guide will help you navigate and use our platform effectively. Whether you're new or experienced, we've got you covered with the information you need.

Features

  • Incident Reporting: Easily submit medical incident reports during the event.
  • Reporting Update: Search by name and update a medical report.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our platform is designed to be intuitive and easy to use.
  • Real-Time Updates: Stay informed with updates related to your incident reports.

How to Use the Platform

To submit a form, follow these simple steps:

  • Refresh the page for a clear form.
  • Fill in the form with the necessary details.
  • Click "Submit" to send your report.

To update a form, follow these simple steps.

  • Search by name of individual.
  • The form will load with their previous information.
  • Update the fields you need.
  • Click "Update" to change the report.
